Resume Genius Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Privacy First: Resume Genie is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store personal information. Here's what happens with your data:

  • Local Storage Only: Your resumes and cover letters are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this content.
  • Anonymous Usage: Basic anonymous analytics may be collected to improve app performance (e.g., app crashes, feature usage), but this data cannot be linked to you personally.
  • Payment Information: If you purchase premium features, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect personal information, we:

  • Do not track your identity or personal details
  • Only process content you explicitly choose to enhance with AI
  • Do not create user profiles or accounts
  • Do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes

3. AI Processing

When you use our AI enhancement features:

  • Temporary Processing: Your resume content is temporarily sent to AI services (OpenAI) for processing
  • No Storage: AI providers process your content but do not store it for training or other purposes (per our agreement)
  • Your Choice: AI features are entirely optional - you can create and edit resumes without ever using AI
  • Immediate Deletion: Content sent for AI processing is deleted immediately after processing

4. Premium Features

Premium subscriptions are managed through:

  • Apple App Store: Subject to Apple's privacy policy

We verify purchases anonymously through receipt validation but do not link receipts to personal identities.

5. Data Security

Your data security is ensured through:

  • All resume data is stored locally on your device
  • AI processing uses encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • No central database of user information
  • You maintain complete control over your data

6. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Local Control: All your resumes are stored on your device and can be deleted at any time
  • No Account Deletion Needed: Since we don't have accounts, there's nothing to delete from our servers
  • Export Your Data: Export your resumes as PDFs at any time
  • Opt Out of AI: Simply don't use AI features if you prefer not to

7. Third-Party Services

Resume Genie may use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: For AI-powered resume enhancement (only when you explicitly request it)
  • App Store: Apple App Store for app distribution and payment processing

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

8. AI-Generated Content Disclaimer

All resume and cover letter content generated by Resume Genie AI is created using artificial intelligence. While we strive for accuracy and quality, AI-generated content may contain errors, inaccuracies, or may not be suitable for all situations.

Please review and edit all AI-generated content before use. Resume Genie is not responsible for the accuracy, completeness, or suitability of AI-generated content for any specific purpose.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. Since we don't collect personal information, we do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@pulsequantumsolutions.com

12. Your Privacy Commitment

We believe in your right to privacy. Resume Genie was built from the ground up to respect your privacy by not collecting unnecessary personal information. Your resume content is yours, stored on your device, and we have no interest in accessing, analyzing, or monetizing it.
